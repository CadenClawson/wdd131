Site Plan: Disc Golf Central

Site Name

Disc Golf Central

The site name represents a hub for disc golf players of all levels. "Disc Golf Central" was chosen because it conveys a central location for disc golf resources, news, and course information.

Optional domain: discgolfcentral.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide disc golf enthusiasts with course directories, beginner guides, equipment reviews, and community updates. It will serve as both an educational resource for new players and a community hub for experienced players.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find disc golf courses near me?
  • What discs are best for beginners starting out?
  • When and where are local tournaments happening?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Dark Green (#2e7d32) – used for headings and navigation bars.

Accent Color: Blue (#1565c0) – used for links, buttons, and highlights.

Background Color: Light Green/Grey (#f4f9f4) – used as page background for readability.

Typography

  • Headings: "Roboto Slab", serif
  • Body Text: "Arial", sans-serif
  • Special Callouts: "Oswald", sans-serif for highlighted stats or banners
